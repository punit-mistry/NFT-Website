About

Mtv Roads is focused on long term, sustainable community building with a Vision to put a positive impact on Truck Drivers in India. Our plan is to create a fund from 100% of proceeds from sales along with a unique royalty scheme which will benefit the holder along with Driver's funds. Driver's funds will be invested in various projects for growth of fund with 100% transparency. Once the funds value is enough, we will setup a Charitable Institution and systematic withdraw it considering Taxation laws , Accounting laws, etc
